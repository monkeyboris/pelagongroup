The Group

Pelagon Group is a specialised trading house connecting regulated markets across Asia and Europe. We operate where complexity is highest and relationships matter most.

We do not own product. We do not move at volume. We build corridors — precise, compliant, and built to last — between producers who need access and buyers who need certainty.

What We Do
I
Import & Export Brokerage

We structure and broker transactions between certified producers and licensed importers. Full documentation management, compliance coordination, and deal execution.

II
Certification Advisory

We guide producers through EU-GMP certification — from gap analysis to site inspection. Long-term retainer engagements for producers serious about European market access.

III
CMO Matching

We connect producers who lack EU-GMP processing capability with the right contract manufacturer. One introduction that unlocks the entire corridor.
