The Group

Pelagon Group is a specialist trading house. We identify commodity opportunities in regulated and emerging markets — and close deals where others cannot navigate the complexity.

We do not hold inventory. We do not require a principal licence. We operate as brokers and advisors — building the corridors, managing the compliance, and executing the transaction.

How We Work

We are not a directory. We are not a database. We are a specialist firm that builds one corridor at a time — with precision, with the right partners, and with long-term stakes in every deal we touch.

I We identify the gap before others see it — and secure the position before it is obvious.
II We never own product. We never require a licence. Revenue comes from brokerage fees, advisory retainers, and deal commissions.
III Every introduction is preceded by qualification. We do not send producers to buyers who cannot receive them.
IV We earn when deals close. Our incentives are fully aligned with the partners we bring to the table.
